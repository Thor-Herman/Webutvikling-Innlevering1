About the artist

Mr. Artist is a very recognized and admired man. He has dedicated the entirety of his existence to the sole purpose of creating graphic content with HTML and SVG. His style is thoroughly influenced by his youth, which he spent great amounts of time surfing the net. When he was 5 years old, he discovered the joy of canvas and SVG, and has never let down his keyboard since. In his most recent collection, you can find his depictions of the human existence.
